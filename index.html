What is mosey?

"Mosey" is how cooperative houses select new members. It is a week of fun events where moseyers can spend time with current house members and learn about the houses. After the events, prospective moseyers will send in applications to be reviewed by the house and new members will be chosen through a weighted lottery system. For fall housing, mosey occurs in early February of the preceding Spring semester.
